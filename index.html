Why UK is the right country for Computer Science

Hey Rahul, let's talk about facts

Quality of Education
Industry-Aligned: Curriculum co-developed with giants like Google and Amazon to master high-demand skills like AI and Cloud.
Placements: Most degrees include industry placements at firms like Google, ARM, or DeepMind.

Time and Cost
